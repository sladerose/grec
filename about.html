Gold Reef Equitation Center’s owner Chris de Klerk has 50 years of experience teaching riders of all ages and abilities, is currently overseeing the horse riding instructors, who have various levels of experience and skills.

We love creating beautiful horses, training beautiful movement,.. but the essence will always be love, which we make available to all, irrespective of social standing or income.

Our farm is located in the idyllic countryside of a small town Umzumbe on the south coast of Kwa Zulu Natal. Our aim is to create a friendly atmosphere and have people return to this relaxing experience. Horses are carefully matched to each rider’s ability allowing safe interaction, care for horses and nurturing genuine love and respect for the animals and sport.

Horse riding is fun! Pupils, particularly children, are taught not only skills on the horse, but skills that are an integral part of good horsemanship and ownership of horses. Whilst learning and teaching we aim to have fun!

Our pony camps are all designed to improve riding skills and ensure our riders are able to compete in basic dressage, as well as showjumping. In between assigned riding rotations, our students work on equestrian theory and will learn the full spectrum of horse and stable care through actual participation in these activities such as stall preparation, feeding, grooming, equipment care, and tacking horses.

Packages for birthday parties and special occasions and beach rides can be arranged.

Private, Group Lessons and Trail rides available.

Our rides are structured toward people looking to spend leisurely, pleasant time on a horse. Out rides are catered to the level of experience of the rider. including adventures through scenic routes, sugar cane and banana plantations, small indigenous forests and crossing rivers. What better way to enjoy South Coast nature than on horseback with your friends and family, with incredible photo opportunities along the route.

Lessons available 7 days a week, which covers show jumping, dressage and general riding skills, with plans available for all ages.

We teach our students and friends balance, responsibly, self-esteem, confidence, kindness, leadership, and develop a genuine love and respect for the animals.
