Pony Camps

Our pony camps are all designed to improve riding skills and ensure our riders are able to compete in basic dressage, as well as showjumping. In between assigned riding rotations, our students work on equestrian theory and will learn the full spectrum of horse and stable care through actual participation in these activities such as stall preparation, feeding, grooming, equipment care, and tacking horses

Riding Lessons

Lessons available 7 days a week, which covers show jumping, dressage, and general riding skills, with plans available for all ages. We teach our students and friends balance, responsibly, self-esteem, confidence, kindness, leadership, and develop a genuine love and respect for the animals

Outrides

Our rides are structured toward people looking to spend leisurely, pleasant time on a horse. Out rides are catered to the level of experience of the rider. including adventures through scenic routes, sugar cane and banana plantations, small indigenous forests and crossing rivers. What better way to enjoy South Coast nature than on horseback with your friends and family, with incredible photo opportunities along the route
